Our Story

SharePlate was founded with the straightforward goal of creating a community where no delicious food is wasted. What began as a modest project among friends has developed into a movement that links volunteers, donors, and local communities throughout the city. The conviction that food is more than just sustenance has driven our journey; it's a means of fostering goodwill, bringing people together, and bringing about positive change. Every action we take, from planning neighborhood food drives to collaborating with shelters and schools, is intended to transform extra meals into memorable experiences. By sharing each plate, we're not only cutting down on waste but also fostering a more cohesive and caring community.

Our Mission

Our goal at SharePlate is to bring people together via the straightforward act of sharing food, turning generosity into a potent and long-lasting force for change. Food, in our opinion, is more than just a source of nourishment; it serves as a conduit between individuals, a means of fostering community, and a means of promoting kindness worldwide. By collaborating with nearby farms, supermarkets, eateries, schools, and shelters to reroute excess food to those in need, we strive every day to minimize food waste while guaranteeing that no meal is wasted.


We do more than just deliver meals. We enable people, organizations, and volunteers to take significant action, transforming modest donations into a cascade that impacts innumerable lives. We want to spread awareness about the importance of food, the effects of waste, and the joy of giving through neighborhood food drives, community events, and awareness campaigns.


In our ideal community, sharing comes naturally, neighbors help one another, and the act of simply offering a plate fosters moments of hope, compassion, and connection. By embracing this goal, SharePlate hopes to create a real impact by promoting sustainability, decreasing hunger, and developing a culture where compassion and generosity are at the core of every meal.


Every plate shared contributes to a world that is more compassionate, interconnected, and waste-free.


Our Vision

At SharePlate, we envision a world where sharing food is second nature, and where no meal goes to waste. We dream of communities where neighbors, friends, and strangers alike come together over a shared plate, fostering connection, understanding, and compassion.


Our vision extends beyond simply providing meals; we want to cultivate a culture of generosity and mindfulness, where people are conscious of the value of food and the impact of waste. By encouraging small acts of sharing, we aim to create a ripple effect that strengthens communities and uplifts individuals.


We aspire to build a future where sustainability and care for others are at the heart of every action, where food systems are smarter and waste is minimized, and where kindness and empathy guide how we share resources. At SharePlate, every shared plate is a step toward a world that is more connected, more compassionate, and more sustainable.


Our Values

  • Community: Together we can make a difference.
  • Sustainability: Reducing waste for a better tomorrow.
