Our Story

SharePlate began with a simple dream — to build a community where no good food goes to waste. We realized how often food is thrown away while others go hungry, so we created a way for people to share what they have and make a difference together.

Our Mission

To connect people through food — encouraging generosity, reducing waste, and creating a sustainable future one plate at a time.

Our Vision

We envision a world where sharing food becomes second nature, and communities come together to support each other with love and care.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every plate shared spreads kindness.
  • Community: Together we can make a difference.
  • Sustainability: Reducing waste for a better tomorrow.
