Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?
You can place an order by browsing the menu, selecting your items, adding them to your cart, and then checking out. You can also follow step-by-step guidance in our chatbot.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery.
Can I track my delivery?
Yes! You can track your order in the 'Orders' section or ask our chatbot for delivery updates.
Do you have any discounts or offers?
Check out the 'Promotions' section to see the latest discounts and offers available.
How do I contact support?
You can contact our support team through the 'Help / Support' section or use the chatbot for immediate assistance.
